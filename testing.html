Careers. Opportunities. Lifestyle.

No need for the summer romance to end!

If youve visited the Marlborough region and fallen in love what we have to offer, why not consider a career move
and come work in Marlborough.

The Marlborough region and local business community is growing.
Were on a global hunt for smart, passionate people like you to help us fill our talent shortage.

In return, were offering a short commute to the office, some of the countries highest sunshine hours, wide open spaces, the sea, skifields, mountain bike tracks and national parks right on your doorstep, an abundance of amazing wineries, cafes and restaurants, friendly local community, great schools, and an iconic events calendar.

Our thriving international industries are looking for professionals across the board, from directors and CEOs, marketing and logistic managers, researchers and data analysts, and much more in between.

Come and see why we love it here.
